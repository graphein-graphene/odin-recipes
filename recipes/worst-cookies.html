Worst Chocolate Chip Cookies

Recipe by Sam on sugarspunrun.com

Description

The worst chocolate cookies. Just...terrible. Who in their right mind would eat these? (58 minutes total time)

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of unsalted butter. Melted, cooled completely.
  • 1 1/2 Cups of light brown sugar. Firmly packed.
  • 1/2 Cup of granulated sugar.
  • 2 Large eggs. Room temperature.
  • 1 Teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  • 1/4 Cup of maple syrup.
  • 3 1/3 Cups of all-purpose flour.
  • 2 Teaspoons of cornstarch.
  • 1 Teaspoon of baking powder.
  • 1 Teaspoon of baking soda.
  • 1 Teaspoon of salt.
  • 2 Cups of chocolate chips.

Steps

In a large bowl:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the melted butter, granulated sugar, and lightbrown sugar.
  2. Add one egg at a time, mixing each one in thoroughly before adding another.
  3. Add in the vanilla extract and maple syrup. Stir thoroughly.

    4. In another bowl:

  4. Whisk the flour, cornstarch, baking powder, baking soda, salt together.

    5. Add this to the other bowl, slowly, mixing thoroughly.

  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Cover bowl with plastic wrap. Chill for 30 minutes (or up to three days for a better appearance and flavor).

    7. After chilling, allow this mixture to sit at room temparature until it is easily scoopable.

  7. While mixture is chilling, preheat oven to 350°F (or 175°C). Line cookie sheets with parchment paper, or use ungreased cookie sheet.
  8. Scoop 2 tablespoons of cookie dough. Roll into ball, making them slightly taller than wide. Repeat this, placing each ball 2 inches apart from one anohter on cookie sheet.
  9. Bake for around 13 minutes, or until edges begin to turn brown.
  10. Cool cookies completely on sheet.

    11. Keep unbaked dough in fridge while waiting. (do not place cookie dough on a hot cookie sheet.)

  11. Upon completing desired number of batches, let cool and enjoy!
