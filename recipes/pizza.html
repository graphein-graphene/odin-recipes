Reuben Pizza

Source Recipe: theskinnyfork.com

Description

Reuben-inspired pizza. Pastrami, swiss cheese, sauerkraut, and thousand-island dressing -- but on pizza.

Ingredients

  • 14 oz. Raw pizza dough
  • 1/4 Cup of light housand island dressing
  • 1/2 Cup of shredded Italian cheese
  • 2 slices of Swiss cheese, cut
  • 1/2 Cup of raw sauerkraut, squeezed dry
  • 1/2 Cup of chopped deli pastrami (or corned beef)
  • Caraway seeds

Steps (summary)

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (or whichever temp your dough specifies). Lay out a piece of parchment paper.
  2. Dust parchment with flour. Roll dough out on paper to form pizza crust.
  3. Spread 1/4 cup of dressing onto crust evenly.
  4. Top with half of the shredded Italian cheese, and the 2 slices of Swiss cheese.
  5. Add the 1/2 cup of raw sauerkraut, 1/2 cup of deli pastrami, and remaining Italian cheese.
  6. Sprinkle the caraway seeds on top.
  7. Finally, place in oven. Bake 10-15 minutes, or until the edges of pizza crust brown and cheese melts.
  8. Remove from oven when done. Enjoy.
